DetailingPro Business

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how DetailingPro Business (the "App") collects, uses, stores and protects data when you use the service.

The App is designed for detailing business operations, including customer management, orders, scheduling, inventory, internal chat and file attachments.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address, authentication identifiers and profile name.
  • Business data you create: customer names, contact details, orders, services, inventory records, calendar data and internal notes.
  • Communication data: team chat messages and attachments uploaded in chat.
  • Media and files: photos and files you choose to upload for business workflows.
  • Technical data: app diagnostics, device-level notification tokens and basic service logs required for reliability and security.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide core app features and save your business data.
  • To synchronize data across authorized devices and team members.
  • To send push and local notifications related to orders and reminders.
  • To protect accounts, prevent abuse and improve service stability.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. Permissions and Device Access

The App may request permissions depending on features you use:

  • Camera and media/gallery access: to add photos and attachments you explicitly choose.
  • Notification permission: to deliver reminders and operational notifications.

You can manage or revoke permissions at any time in your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Firebase services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging

These providers process data under their own security and compliance frameworks.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Data may be shared only in the following cases:

  • Within your authorized organization/team in the App.
  • With infrastructure providers required to operate the service.
  • When required by law or valid legal process.

7. Data Retention

Data is retained while your account remains active and as needed to provide the service.

You may request deletion of account-related data. Some records may be retained where required by law, fraud prevention or legitimate dispute handling.

8. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, loss or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Children

The App is intended for business users and is not directed to children under 13 (or higher age threshold where required by local law). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export, restrict or delete personal data. To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page with a new effective date.
